About Glenjoza

Introduction

Glenjoza Projects & Management (Pty) Ltd is a registered and compliant construction and civil engineering company delivering turnkey building, infrastructure, supply and delivery solutions to public and private sector clients.

We collaborate with key stakeholders across various industries to provide cost-effective, reliable and high-quality services rooted in integrity, value for money and timely project delivery.

Ownership: 100% Black-Owned Enterprise

Industry Focus: Civil Engineering & Construction

Ownership & Empowerment

Glenjoza Projects & Management (Pty) Ltd is a 100% black-owned and managed enterprise committed to contributing meaningfully to South Africa’s infrastructure development and transformation objectives.

As the company grows, we aim to create sustainable employment opportunities by engaging skilled professionals and developing emerging talent from leading institutions across the country. We place particular emphasis on expanding opportunities for young professionals and supporting greater participation of women within the construction and civil engineering sector.

Our objective is to contribute to a more inclusive and capable industry while delivering infrastructure solutions that support long-term national growth.

Our Vision

To become a trusted contractor in construction, civil works, renovation, maintenance and infrastructure delivery through disciplined execution and professional management.
