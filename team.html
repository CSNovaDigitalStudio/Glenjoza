Meet the Team

Glenton Mashau Makobololo

Director

As the founder and Director of Glenjoza Projects & Management, Glenton Makobololo brings entrepreneurial leadership and hands-on technical expertise to the company.

His career began with owning Glen Health Corner where he developed strong operational, sales and logistics management skills. These experiences built the discipline required to run a successful enterprise.

He holds certifications in roadside safety procedures and road traffic sign manufacturing from SARTSMA, training in roadworks traffic accommodation (SARF), and the Occupational Health & Safety Act.

As Director, he ensures every infrastructure project is executed with strict adherence to safety regulations, legislative compliance and the highest standards of quality.

Desire Cathrine Makobolola

Operations Manager

Desire Makobolola is responsible for the operational execution of projects at Glenjoza Projects & Management, ensuring that work on site meets the company's standards for safety, quality and efficiency.

With over a decade of experience she has worked in large-scale construction environments including Charlotte Maxeke Hospital projects and EPWP programs.

Her experience as an Occupational Health and Safety Representative at Nweti Construction strengthened her ability to enforce strict safety protocols including PPE compliance, toolbox talks and site safety management.

She ensures disciplined project execution, resource management and smooth coordination of all site activities.

Ngozi Shade Okoro-Obong

Account Manager & Growth Strategist

Ngozi Shade Okoro-Obong leads Glenjoza’s strategic growth and market positioning.

She holds a BA Honours in Strategic Communication from the University of Johannesburg and previously worked at Yellowwood Future Architects (TBWA\SA).

Her expertise includes trend analysis, go-to-market strategy, and crafting value propositions for major brands including MTN and Nedbank.

At Glenjoza she identifies new opportunities, develops client relationships and ensures sustainable business growth through data-driven strategy and market insights.
